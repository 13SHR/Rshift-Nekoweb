A disturbingly beautiful chaotic end of the world

Hello everyone, welcome to my review about Dead D... you read the title already. I've had the manga on my planning list for a long moment now, and intend to read it later because I loved the ONA !

The story of the manga is quite complex, two girls live in Japan, where aliens parked their mother ship, and start to send explorers on Earth. Around this plot comes some time travelling and such complicated schemes, which I won't enter in detail to avoid spoiling huge revelations.
The episodes are given in a seemingly random order, that is not chronological, what creates kind of a mess when trying to remember everything that happened, so it helped to binge the show in 2 days. This achronological things sets up the atmosphere of the story : chaos.

Everything is chaos. Alien Rubik's cubes and teapots are falling from the sky. The characters have random behaviour, they all say and do weird things just because it makes them laugh, and they all act like it's perfectly normal. While it creates really fun scenes, it also helps to create a very big contrast with serious moments, and even more with dramatic ones.
The author uses death and sorrow, as well as horror, to progress toward the literal end of humanity. This descent into hell is so much efficient emotionally that I found myself "depressed" after watching some episodes, as if my own world was going to collapse.
The most difficult moment to handle was the death of Kiho, that created a before/after, and started the disappearance of joy in their life.

Every character is unique, having a strong visual identity, a different personality, a different goal in life and everything. Seeing the amount of characters appearing, it is really impressing to see so many well thought people being alive.
This work is helped by the incredible voice acting behind them, that help getting the mood of the character.

The manga is know for its 3D-based graphics, and I saw some of them, seeing beautiful details and machines. So I expected the anime to mimic this style, and it did even better, adding light play and transparency kana. This is, in my opinion, a perfect example of how to use 3D as a meaningful feature, that adds a complete other dimension to the world, which is the one of aliens.

Conclusion

I am not used to enjoy anime that much, the artistic choices help to complete the original manga, and really create an impressive work. I can't wait to discover the story again in the manga ! 10/10
